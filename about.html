OUR STORY

Health & Innovation

Born from a shared frustration with reactive wound care, MediPulse was founded to make invisible healing visible — and to put the power of real-time data in every clinician's hands.

OUR MISSION

Preventing Complications Before They Begin

Every year, over 8 million patients in the US alone develop chronic wounds. Hospital-acquired infections drive billions in avoidable costs. MediPulse was built on a single conviction: that continuous, intelligent wound monitoring can transform patient outcomes.

We combine materials science, biosensor engineering, and clinical AI to create smart dressings that think — alerting clinicians to infection, tracking healing velocity, and guiding data-driven interventions.
